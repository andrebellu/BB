About us

Swan B&B is a charming bed and breakfast located in a scenic countryside that offers comfortable and tastefully decorated rooms for solo travelers, couples, friends or colleagues traveling together. The B&B provides a range of amenities, including free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a complimentary breakfast every morning. It is the perfect getaway for those seeking peace and relaxation away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
